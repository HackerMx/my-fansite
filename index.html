About

Stray Kids (SKZ) is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name. The group consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.

They debuted on March 25, 2018, with their extended play (EP) "I Am NOT". Known for their powerful performances, self-produced music, and meaningful lyrics, Stray Kids have quickly risen to fame both in South Korea and internationally.

Some of their popular songs include "God's Menu", "Back Door", "Thunderous", and "MANIAC". Stray Kids are also known for their unique concepts and strong connection with their fans, known as Stays.
